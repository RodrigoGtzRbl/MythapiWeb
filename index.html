The API of Mythology

What You Can Find

  • Mythical Characters: Get information about heroes and legendary figures from different mythologies, including details about their powers, abilities, and family relationships.
  • Mythical Creatures: Explore fantastic creatures and monsters from diferent mythologies, with descriptions of their powers and relations.
  • Gods and Deities: Access data about gods and deities from various mythologies, with descriptions of their powers and relations.
  • Heroes: Discover legendary heroes from various mythologies, with descriptions of their powers and relations.
  • Mythical Artifacts: Consult details about magical objects and mythical artifacts, including their powers and relations.
  • Myths: Consult basic details about myths from various mythologies, incluiding description, hero's name from the myth, creatures and artifacts involved.

How to Use

    Using the name of the archive and the ID/name of the object to search

  • GET /archive/all
  • Lists all characters.
  • GET /archive/id/:id
  • Retrieves details of a specific character using their ID.
  • GET /archive/name/:name
  • Retrieves details of a specific character using their name.

    • Using only name of the object to search

  • GET /search/:name
  • Retrieves details of a specific character using their name.
